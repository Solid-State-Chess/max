#include <move.h>

Detailed Description

A list of (optionally packed) moves filled during movegen and searched by the engine.

Because the engine must perform move ordering to maximize the number of pruned nodes during a search, the full list of every possible move at every depth level must be recorded - potentially representing a significant amount of memory usage.
