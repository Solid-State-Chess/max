0x88 Square Differences
Chessboard » Board Location Indices

Detailed Description

The difference between two 0x88 board locations is unique with respect to distance and direction, allowing us to map square differences to arrays very compactly.

This is most often used for check and pin detection, as it's very easy to determine if two squares lie on the same diagonal or cardinal ray.
