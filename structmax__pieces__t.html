max_0x88_t max_pieces_t::initial_rook[MAX_CASTLES_LEN]

Initial positions of the A and H side rooks.

For chess960, the two rooks must always be left (A side) and right (H side) of the king, but they may start on any square. In order to track castling rights and compute castling validity, we must know the initial square of the rook
